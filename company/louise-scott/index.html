Kennedy Space Centre

Friday 11th January 2008

In November 2007 I visited Florida for a holiday with my partner and children. During our holiday we visited the Kennedy Space Centre. It was a fantastic day, the space centre has so many attractions to see and experience, one day is not enough.

The best part was watching the work force building the space station. You find it hard to believe the parts you see being made will soon be floating around in space for many years.
Seeing the Space Shuttle ready for launch was also very exciting, it may have been a few miles away but still breathtaking.
